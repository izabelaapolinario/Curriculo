Corporate Intranet

Núcleo Assistencial Caminhos para Jesus is a social assistance organization driven by love and donations. Its mission is to provide a dignified life and excellent care to its residents through dedication, compassion, and a well-structured environment.

The system I developed for the organization includes several essential features for efficient management:

1. Authentication and Security:

Users can securely change their passwords.
Access control based on permission levels ensures data confidentiality.

2. Employee Registration and Editing:

Employees can be registered with detailed information such as name, position, contact, and personal data.
Information can be updated as needed.

3. Medication Management:

Registration of medications available at the institution.
Edit and delete medications as needed.
Strict control to ensure medications are always up to date and available.

4. Medication and Meal Requests:

Staff can request medications and meals for the residents.
The system automatically calculates the total cost based on requests.

5. Reports:

Medication inventory report to monitor availability and avoid shortages.
Employee report with detailed team information.
Financial report including data on expenses, revenues, and budgets.

6. Charity Bazaar:

Implementation of a bazaar module to register donated or acquired items.
Inventory control for clothing, footwear, household items, and other products.
Tracking of donations and sales, including value calculations.

7. Patient Registration:

Ability to register patients assisted by the organization.
Detailed information such as name, age, medical history, and specific needs.
Assignment of responsible caregivers to each patient.

8. Appointment Scheduling:

Scheduling of medical appointments, therapies, and other services.
Automatic notifications for patients and staff with scheduled dates and times.

9. Volunteer Management:

Registration of volunteers interested in contributing to the organization.
Task assignment and tracking of completed activities.

10. Event and Activity Management:

Recording of events such as fundraising campaigns, lectures, and workshops.
Participant and resource tracking for each event.

11. Internal Communication:

Internal messaging between staff, volunteers, and patients.
Notifications about updates, events, and important information.

This system supports the operational efficiency of Núcleo Assistencial Caminhos para Jesus, enabling the team to manage resources, meet residents’ needs, and maintain an environment of care and compassion.
